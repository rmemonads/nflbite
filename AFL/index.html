The Australian Football League (AFL) is one of the most exciting and fast-paced sports competitions in the world. Originating in Australia, AFL blends the skills of football, rugby, and athletics into a unique game that has gained millions of passionate fans both locally and internationally.

What is AFL?

AFL stands for Australian Football League, the top professional competition of Australian Rules Football. The league began in 1896 as the Victorian Football League (VFL) before expanding nationally in 1990, becoming the AFL we know today.

  • Number of teams: 18
  • Competition format: Regular season + Finals Series
  • Grand Final: Played annually at the Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG) in front of 100,000+ fans
  • Most successful clubs: Collingwood, Essendon, Carlton, Hawthorn, Richmond

Why AFL is Unique

  • Oval-shaped field instead of rectangular
  • High-scoring nature with goals worth 6 points
  • Physical contact + nonstop running
  • Spectacular marks (high catches) that thrill fans

It’s often described as the fastest game on grass, making it a must-watch for sports fans.

How AFL Scoring Works

AFL’s scoring system is simple but unique:

  • Goal (6 points): When the ball is kicked between the two tall goalposts without being touched.
  • Behind (1 point): When the ball passes between a tall and a short post, or touches a player before crossing the goal line.

This means matches can have very high scores, often exceeding 100 points per team.

AFL and Major Rivalries

  • Carlton vs Collingwood – one of the oldest rivalries in Australian sport.
  • Essendon vs Hawthorn – fierce contests in the 1980s and 1990s.
  • Adelaide Crows vs Port Adelaide Power – the famous “Showdown.”
  • Sydney Swans vs GWS Giants – the “Battle of the Bridge.”

AFL Grand Final – The Biggest Event in Australia

The AFL Grand Final is the pinnacle of the season. Played at the Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG), it regularly draws over 100,000 spectators and millions of TV viewers.

  • National anthem & pre-game entertainment
  • Halftime show with Australian music stars
  • Presentation of the Premiership Cup
  • The awarding of the Norm Smith Medal for best player in the Grand Final

For many Australians, the AFL Grand Final is as big as the Super Bowl in the USA.

AFL Women’s League (AFLW)

The AFL Women’s League (AFLW) has grown rapidly since its launch in 2017. Featuring top female athletes, AFLW continues to inspire a new generation of fans and promote gender equality in sport.

AFL vs Other Sports

  • AFL vs NFL: Both are physical, but NFL is stop-start, while AFL is continuous.
  • AFL vs Soccer: AFL is higher-scoring and played on an oval field.
  • AFL vs Rugby: AFL has more kicking, catching, and athletic marking.

This uniqueness gives AFL its global appeal.
