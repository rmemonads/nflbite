Common Questions About Fixtures & Schedules

How early are fixtures posted?

Major competitions usually release full-season calendars months in advance, but kick-off times and TV slots are frequently finalized closer to match dates.

Why does my broadcast list show different times?

Broadcasters might list local market times or include pre-game coverage windows; always check the official match center for the confirmed kick-off time.

Can I automatically sync fixtures to my phone?

Yes. Use the Add-to-Calendar feature (iCal/Google) on your fixtures page or subscribe to an official league calendar feed.
